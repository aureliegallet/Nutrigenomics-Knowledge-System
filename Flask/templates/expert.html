Our Expert



Michael Müller


Professor Emeritus of Nutrigenomics and Systems Nutrition


Norwich Medical School, University of East Anglia



  Researcher Profile: ORCID





Müller has been a full professor for roughly 25 years. Müller's recent research has been on the study of how nutrition influences gene expression and metabolic pathways. Müller's work has generally been on the molecular mechanisms by which nutrients and bioactive food components affect health. Furthermore, Müller has researched the gut-brain axis and the relationship it has with general health. Our expert system will reflect and combine Müller's varied areas of expertise.
